Privacy at a glance

Privacy is a core principle, not an afterthought. Here is the short version of what we collect and why.

No browsing data collected

The extension never sends your browsing history, URLs, or page content to any server. All lookups happen locally against a cached database stored on your device.

No third-party analytics

No Google Analytics, no tracking pixels, no fingerprinting. We don't know who you are, what sites you visit, or how you use the extension.

No account needed

Rating a tool requires no login, no email, no account. Submissions are anonymous -- we store the rating data but not who sent it (your IP is briefly held in a separate rate-limit log, never longer than ~1 hour). The optional newsletter signup is the one place we store an email, and only so we can send you the weekly picks. Unsubscribe anytime.

Open source and auditable

The full source code is public on GitHub. The ratings database is public. Anyone can verify exactly what data the extension accesses and stores.
